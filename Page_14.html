Guideline 1: Neonates Have an Immature Immune System

The body has a unique system of defense designed to protect against Iinfections and foreign substances. Exposure to an infectious agent activates a complex series of responses to identify, neutralize, and eliminate the invading organism.

Neonates have an immature immune system and an impaired ability to effectively eliminate invading organisms.

Preterm infants are at an even greater disadvantage than term infants. The younger the gestation, the higher the risk for infection and an adverse outcome.
